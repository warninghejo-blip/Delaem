=== ABOUT FENNEC SWAP ===
Fennec Swap is a non-custodial DEX and terminal for the Fractal Bitcoin ecosystem.
You keep full custody: transactions are signed in your UniSat wallet.

CORE FEATURES:
- SWAP: Trade FB ↔ FENNEC and BTC ↔ FB.
- DEPOSIT: Bring BTC into Fractal (it appears as BTC inside Fractal).
- WITHDRAW: Return funds from Fractal back to Bitcoin mainnet.
- FENNEC ID: A user card that summarizes on-chain activity and achievements.
- TERMINAL: Price, history, and trading tools.

=== FRACTAL BITCOIN QUICK NOTES ===
- Fast blocks (~30 seconds).
- Uses Bitcoin-style addresses and keys.
- FB is the main asset / gas in the UI (sFB___000).
- BTC in the UI is bridged BTC inside Fractal (sBTC___000).

=== FENNEC ID QUICK NOTES ===
- Fennec ID is generated from wallet-derived metrics (activity, wealth, time, badges).
- Rarity thresholds:
  - CUB: 0-29
  - SCOUT: 30-49
  - HUNTER: 50-64
  - ALPHA: 65-79
  - ELDER: 80-94
  - SPIRIT: 95-100

=== SAFETY ===
- Never share your seed phrase.
- Only sign transactions you understand.
- Verify you are on the correct domain.

=== TROUBLESHOOTING ===
- If something fails, try a hard refresh and check the browser console.
- Rate limiting (429) means you should wait 10-30 seconds.
- If upstream services are down, some data may be incomplete temporarily.
